What you get

Built for how real households actually work

Most budgeting apps assume one person, one income. Owó is designed from the ground up for shared finances.

🏠
Household Members

Add your partner, roommates, or family. Assign income per person and track contributions side-by-side. Each person keeps their own private view.

📂
Category Budgets

Set budgets for groceries, transport, dining, and any custom category. See at a glance what's on track, overspent, or under-used — with colour-coded progress bars.

💸
Income Distribution

Automatically calculates how leftover income should be split across savings and investments. Mark each allocation as done and track what's been moved.

📋
Debt Ledger

Track what you owe and what's owed to you. Standalone debts with APR auto-calculate interest monthly. Account debts appear automatically when an account goes negative.

🔁
Recurring Entries

Set up rent, subscriptions, and regular income once. They auto-populate each period so your baseline is always ready without lifting a finger.

📊
Summary & Charts

Month-over-month spending charts, category breakdowns, and all-time summaries. Filter by day, week, month, or year to spot patterns and stay on track.

Getting started

Up and running in minutes

No imports. No bank linking. No complexity. Just open it and start budgeting.

01
Set up your household

Enter the names of the people in your household and their income. Invite others by email to join a shared household view — or keep it just for you.

02
Log your transactions

Add expenses, income, and transfers as they happen. Set recurring entries for the things that never change. Categorise everything with a tap.

03
See the full picture

Your budget, accounts, debts, and savings — all updated in real time. At month end, see exactly what's left and where it should go.
